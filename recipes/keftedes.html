Keftedes

Description

Simple delicius meatballs.

Ingridients

  • 350g Ground Beef
  • 150g Ground pork
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 egg
  • 20g Stale Bread
  • Dried oregano, cumin, paprika

Instructions

  1. Mix the meat with the rest of ingridients.
  2. Let it rest in the fridge for an hour.
  3. Form the meat mixture into balls. To a pan add oil up to about half the height of your meatballs.
  4. Heat the pan and add you meatballs as to not crowd the pan too much.
  5. Cook until a nice crust has formed on one side then flip. When cooked all the way throught place on a wire rack and let cool.
