Dry Manhattan

Description

A boozy drink ideal for someone that does not like sweeter coctails.

Ingridients

  • 60ml Rye of Bourbon
  • 30ml Dry Vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Lemon for garnish
  • Ice for chilling

Instructions

  1. In a coctail shaker filled with ice add all the ingredients.
  2. Stir together until combined about 30 seconds.
  3. Strain into a coctail glass.
  4. Twist a lemon peel to release the essential oils over the drink.
