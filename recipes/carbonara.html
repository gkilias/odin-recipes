Creamless Carbonara

Description

Simple creamy pasta with only 4 ingridients. The cheese gives a decadent creamy texture that comes together with the salty pork.

Ingridients

  • 50g Pecorino
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 100g Guanciale or Pancetta or Bacon
  • 4 eggs (3 egg yolks 1 whole)

Instructions

  1. Heavily salt water and bring it to a rolling boil. When the water boils add the pasta.
  2. While waiting for the water to boil chop the guanciale into small cubes and grate the cheese.
  3. Whisk together the cheese and eggs. Add a lot of peper.
  4. Add the guanciale to a cold pan. Set the heat to medium and cook until crisp. Remove and set aside. Reserve some of the fat.
  5. When the pasta has been boiling 2 mins less than package insturctions drain the pasta, reserve some of the pasta water and add it to the egg mixture until you get a creamy texture. Add the reserved fat back into the pan then add the pasta.
  6. Over low heat add the egg mixture into the pan with the pasta and stir continuosly, add pasta water if required until the pasta becomes creamy.
